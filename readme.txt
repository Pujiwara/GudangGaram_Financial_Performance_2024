PT Gudang Garam Tbk (GGRM) is one of Indonesia’s largest cigarette manufacturers.
This analysis reviews the company’s financial performance from 2020 to 2024 to address key questions commonly raised by management, investors, and finance teams.

The available datasets include the Balance Sheet, Income Statement, and Cash Flow Statement for the years 2020–2024.

Key Business Questions
1. What are the trends in GGRM’s revenue and net income over the past five years? Have profit margins (gross, operating, and net) improved or deteriorated?
2. How significant was the decline in sales in 2024 compared to the previous year?
3. What were the main drivers behind the sharp drop in net income in 2024 — declining sales, rising expenses, or other factors?
4. Is the operating cash flow aligned with net income trends?
5. Has GGRM consistently generated positive cash flow from operations?
6. Does the company rely on external financing (debt) or internal cash generation to fund investments?
7. What are the trends in cash usage for investment activities and interest payments?
8. How have total assets, liabilities, and equity evolved over the past five years?
9. Is the capital structure healthy (proportion of debt vs. equity financing)?
10. What are the trends in GGRM’s Debt-to-Equity Ratio (DER) and Current Ratio?
11. Is the company’s liquidity position improving or weakening?
12. How has Return on Equity (ROE) trended over the five-year period?
